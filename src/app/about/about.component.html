Crafting Flavors, Creating Memories

Welcome to Whistle Street! We're a company based in Makati, Philippines, specializing in making delicious cocktail and mocktail mixes. We started our journey in May 2024. Our goal is simple: we want to help people discover new flavors and create their own amazing drinks using our products. Whether you're into cocktails or prefer non-alcoholic mocktails, we've got something for everyone. What makes us unique is our commitment to quality and supporting local farmers. We use only the best ingredients sourced directly from nearby farms, which not only makes our mixes taste great but also helps our community thrive. We couldn't do it without the help of cocktail and mocktail enthusiasts, as well as professional bartenders. Their expertise and feedback help us create mixes that are both delicious and easy to use. So whether you're a seasoned bartender or just starting out, come visit us at our shop in Makati and let us help you find your new favorite drink. Cheers to good times and great flavors at Whistle Street!
